Email Signature Guidance

Use this tool to generate a standardized Civil Air Patrol email signature block.

Guidelines, Standards, and Requirements

  • Use approved CAP grade abbreviations. Do not use “SM.”
  • Do not include professional titles or post-nominals.
  • List no more than two duty assignments recorded in eServices.
  • Do not append quotes, disclaimers, or unofficial titles.
  • Review brand standards warnings before copying output.
