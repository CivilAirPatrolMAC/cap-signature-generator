Email Signature Guidance

To generate your email signature, you can use this tool, copy from approved samples, or use the Word template on the brand portal.

Guidelines, Standards, and Requirements

  • Use approved CAP grade abbreviations. “SM” is not a grade and should not be used.
  • Do not include professional titles or post-nominals.
  • List no more than two duty assignments recorded in eServices.
  • Do not append quotes, disclaimers, or unofficial titles.
  • Do not alter the signature formatting beyond your personal details.

Signature Type

Default is recommended for desktop email clients. Plain Text is best for clients that strip formatting. Abbreviated (Mobile) is intended for mobile devices only.

Grade & Name

Use approved CAP grade abbreviations. Do not use “SM” or add professional titles or post-nominals.

Use 4-letter Wing, 3-letter Region, or “HQ”; no squadron designators. Used only for the abbreviated mobile signature.

Duty Position(s)

Unit name followed by duty position. Maximum two lines allowed.

List no more than two duty assignments recorded in eServices. Organize assignments from highest to lowest organizational level.

Phone Number(s)

Phone numbers are automatically formatted with periods as you type.

Wing/Region Website

Optional. Include only official Wing or Region websites relevant to your role.

How to use your signature

  1. Build and review your signature in Rendered preview mode.
  2. Switch to Code if your email client requires HTML source.
  3. Copy the finished signature into your email client’s signature settings.
  4. Send a test email to yourself and confirm spacing, links, and logo appearance.
  5. On mobile devices, use the Abbreviated (Mobile) version instead of the full HTML signature.
